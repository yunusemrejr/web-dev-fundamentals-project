Fun Facts

Fact 1: Dolphins Sleep with One Eye Open

Dolphins are fascinating creatures that sleep with one eye open. They do this because only one hemisphere of their brain sleeps at a time while the other remains awake to keep them alert to predators and other dangers.

Fact 2: Tomatoes Are Technically a Fruit

While often considered a vegetable, tomatoes are technically a fruit because they develop from the ovary of a flower and contain seeds. This makes them a fruit by botanical definition, despite their common culinary use as a vegetable.

Fact 3: Flamingos Are Born Gray, Not Pink

Flamingos are born with gray feathers, not pink. Their iconic pink color comes from the carotenoid pigments in their diet of algae, shrimp, and crustaceans. The more they eat, the pinker they become!

Fact 4: Elephants Are the Only Mammals That Can't Jump

Elephants are the only mammals that cannot jump. Their legs have evolved for carrying their massive weight, and while they can run, they lack the ability to lift all four feet off the ground simultaneously.

Fact 5: The Eiffel Tower Can Grow in Summer

Due to the expansion of iron in the heat, the Eiffel Tower can grow more than 6 inches during hot summer days. This phenomenon is known as thermal expansion, where materials expand when heated and contract when cooled.
